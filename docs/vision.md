# Project Lakshay

## Vision

Project Lakshay is a Personal Wealth Operating System designed to help people make better financial decisions through intelligent planning, goal tracking, and actionable financial insights.

Unlike traditional expense trackers, Lakshay focuses on answering one simple question:

> "What should I do next to improve my financial life?"

---

## Mission

Help users achieve financial independence through data-driven planning and AI-assisted decision making.

---

## Product Principles

1. Decision over Data
2. Goals over Transactions
3. Explainability over Magic
4. Simplicity over Complexity
5. Monthly Planning over Daily Tracking

---

## North Star

Every user should know:

- Their Financial Health Score
- Why it is that score
- How to improve it

within 60 seconds of opening the app.
